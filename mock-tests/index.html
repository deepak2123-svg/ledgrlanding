How it works

From question setup to result review in four steps.

Mock Tests keeps each role separate, but the work still moves through one clear publishing flow.

Step

Teacher sets up the paper.

Teachers import questions, review parsed MCQs, tag subject/chapter details and prepare the test bank.

Teacher Workspace
Step

Admin publishes and controls access.

Admins choose timing, attempts, visibility and result-release rules before assigning a test.

Admin Console
Step

Student attempts the mock test.

Students enter their own portal, attempt the timed test and submit without seeing teacher/admin tools.

Student Portal
Step

Results are reviewed and exported.

Admins and teachers can review attempts, spot weak chapters and export results for follow-up.

Student experience

A focused test room, not a generic login page.

Students see test availability, timed attempt state, subject-level performance and released results without entering teacher or admin tools.

  • Timed NDA, JEE and NEET practice shells.
  • Attempt progress and released result review.
  • Question-level flagging for teacher follow-up.
Teacher and admin workflow

Question import, publishing and results stay organized.

Teachers can move content into a structured bank, while admins keep the publishing queue and result review under control.

  • Question-bank import for teachers.
  • Draft, review and publish states for admins.
  • Result snapshots by test, attempt count and average score.
