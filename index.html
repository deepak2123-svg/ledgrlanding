What Ledgr replaces

Less chasing. More control over academic delivery.

Coaching centres run on many small daily signals. Ledgr collects those signals while the work is happening, so management can act before the month is over.

Class logging

A live academic record for every classroom.

Teachers get a fast daily logging workflow. Directors get a control centre that shows what happened, what is pending, and where the institute needs follow-up.

From teacher entry to institute report.

Ledgr captures class activity at the source, connects it to institutes, sections, subjects and syllabus progress, then turns the work into operational visibility for admins.

  • Teachers log class topic, timing, status, notes and syllabus progress.
  • Admins manage teachers, institutes, sections, syllabus and access.
  • Ledgr Report creates daily and institute-wise summaries for review.
  • Feedback and follow-up loops keep teachers and admins aligned.
